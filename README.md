# ☕ Starbucks Sales Analytics

An end-to-end data analytics project that explores customer purchasing behavior, digital channel adoption, operational efficiency, and revenue drivers using a simulated Starbucks dataset containing 100,000 transactions from 2024–2025.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

Modern coffee shop operate across multiple ordering channels including Mobile App, Drive-Thru, In-Store, and Kiosk. Understanding customer behavior across these channels is essential for improving operational efficiency, increasing customer satisfaction, and maximizing revenue.

This project analyzes Starbucks ordering data to uncover business insights through exploratory data analysis, customer segmentation, predictive modeling, and interactive dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

This project aims to transform transactional retail data into actionable business insights by addressing the following objectives:

1. **Analyze Channel Migration**
   - Quantify the shift from traditional ordering channels (**In-Store** and **Drive-Thru**) to digital channels (**Mobile App** and **Kiosk**).
   - Identify customer segments based on **age group** and **region** that exhibit lower mobile adoption rates, enabling targeted digital engagement strategies.

2. **Build Customer Segments**
   - Segment customers using **Recency, Frequency, Monetary (RFM)** analysis or **K-Means Clustering**.
   - Identify high-value customer personas, such as **Coffee Enthusiasts** and **Morning Commuters**, to support personalized marketing and customer retention initiatives.

3. **Develop Predictive Revenue Models**
   - Build regression models to predict customer spending (`total_spend`) based on operational and behavioral variables, including **time of day**, **store location type**, and **product customization counts**.
   - Understand the key drivers that influence transaction value.

4. **Identify Operational Bottlenecks**
   - Evaluate order fulfillment performance across different ordering channels.
   - Analyze `fulfillment_time_min` to identify operational inefficiencies, particularly during **peak morning hours**, and recommend process improvements.

5. **Evaluate Loyalty Program Effectiveness**
   - Assess the impact of the **Starbucks Rewards** program on customer behavior.
   - Measure how membership status influences **basket size**, **purchase frequency**, **customer satisfaction**, and overall customer value.

6. **Optimize Menu Strategy**
   - Analyze relationships between beverage categories and customization behavior.
   - Identify opportunities for introducing or expanding **Mobile-Only** menu offerings that could increase revenue and enhance the digital ordering experience.

## 📈 Key Insights

Examples of insights generated from this project include:

- Mobile App users spend more per transaction.
- Rewards members have higher average basket values.
- Drive-Thru experiences longer fulfillment times during morning rush.
- Customers aged 25–34 show the highest digital adoption.
- Beverage customization positively correlates with transaction value.
